Legal Status and Crypto Regulation in Bali

Crypto is not legal tender in Indonesia but trading and exchanging are allowed. Local laws require service providers to comply with anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism rules. Cashing out is generally tolerated but always check for latest updates and stay compliant, especially for large deals.

Where to find regulatory updates?

  • BAPPEBTI (Indonesian Commodity Futures Trading Regulatory Agency)
  • OJK (Financial Services Authority)
